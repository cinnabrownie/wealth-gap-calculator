Account reference ▾
Account Tax treatment 2026 limit Worth knowing
401(k) / 403(b) Pre-tax or Roth option $24,500 Contribute at least enough to capture the employer match — that's an immediate 50–100% return on those dollars.
Roth IRA After-tax in, tax-free out $7,500 Income limit phases out above $153K single / $242K married. Best used when you expect to be in a higher bracket later.
Traditional IRA Tax-deferred $7,500 Deductibility depends on income and whether you have a workplace plan. Taxed as ordinary income on withdrawal.
HSA Pre-tax in, tax-free growth, tax-free out $4,400 ind. / $8,750 fam. Requires a high-deductible health plan. Invest the balance — it becomes a stealth retirement account after 65.
Brokerage Taxable gains No limit Long-term capital gains rate applies if held over one year — meaningfully lower than ordinary income tax.
Other Varies Varies REITs, I-bonds, real estate equity, and similar. Include any vehicle where your money is working rather than sitting.
